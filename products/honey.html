Tullamore D.E.W. Honey

Irish Whiskey Liqueur

Tullamore D.E.W. Honey combines the smooth taste of our triple distilled, triple blend Irish whiskey with the sweetness of all-natural honey. This perfectly balanced liqueur delivers the authentic character of Irish whiskey with a touch of golden sweetness.

Created using our signature blend of grain, malt, and pot still whiskeys, each sip delivers the warmth and complexity of traditional Irish whiskey enhanced by the natural sweetness of carefully selected honey. Perfect for sipping neat, on the rocks, or as a cocktail ingredient.
