Tullamore D.E.W. 14 Year Old Single Malt

Four Cask Matured Irish Single Malt

Our 14 Year Old Single Malt represents the ultimate expression of Tullamore D.E.W.'s single malt whiskey-making expertise. Crafted exclusively from malted barley and matured for 14 years in four distinct cask types for exceptional depth and character.

This expression showcases the intricate interplay between traditional Irish single malt production and innovative cask finishing techniques. Each cask contributes unique characteristics, creating a symphony of flavors that exemplifies our commitment to excellence.
