Tullamore D.E.W. 12 Year Old

Special Reserve Irish Whiskey

Tullamore D.E.W. 12 Year Old Special Reserve represents the pinnacle of our blending expertise. This exceptional whiskey is aged for a minimum of 12 years and finished in four different cask types: refill bourbon barrels, port wine casks, Madeira casks, and sherry butts.

This innovative four-cask maturation process creates unparalleled depth and complexity, with each cask type contributing distinct characteristics. The result is a harmonious blend that showcases the full potential of Irish whiskey craftsmanship.
